About Me

I have always found a great interest in the world of technology and how it shapes our lives. From web development to the development of AI, I want to be a part of the evolution where technology can improve peoples' lives.

After I graduated from the University of Lethbridge (Calgary Campus), with a Bachelor of Management degree in HR, I believed that it was my true calling to help improve and excel employees in a company. As time went on, I found that my true calling was web & software development. I immediately found it very interesting, and fell in love with it ever since.

Eventually, I would like to become a full stack developer and learn the back-end development programs like Python, C#, and SQL or get into cyber security in the near future.

During my leisure time, I like to watch documentaries, read books, solving the Rubik's cube, travel, and enjoy the mountains and nature.
